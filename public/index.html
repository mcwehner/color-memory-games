How it works

1 Observe

A random color from the OKLab gamut fills your screen for five seconds. Take it in.

2 Recreate

The color disappears. Use the perceptual picker to dial in what you remember.

3 Score

See your original and pick side by side, scored on the true perceptual distance between them.

Perceptually honest scoring

Unlike RGB or HSL, OKLab is designed so that equal numerical distances correspond to equal perceived differences. Your score is the Euclidean distance between your pick and the target — a true measure of how close your memory was to reality.
